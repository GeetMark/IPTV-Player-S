# IPTV Player S


**The IPTV Player S application provides an ideal solution for those seeking a comprehensive viewing experience with IPTV, EPG (Electronic Program Guide), VOD (Video on Demand), Video Series, and Catch-up TV, all conveniently accessible on Android devices.**

**With this application, you can enjoy not only IPTV content from your Internet Service Provider but also access a wide range of free live TV channels from various sources available on the internet.**

## Key Features:

Extensive Support: The application supports both M3U and Xtream-Codes API Playlist, catering to diverse playlist formats.
Integrated IPTV Player: A robust built-in IPTV player is provided, enhancing your playback experience.
Chromecast Compatibility: Seamlessly stream content to your Chromecast-enabled devices for larger screen enjoyment.
Versatile Network Protocols: The application supports multiple network protocols including FTP, HTTP, MMS, RSS/Atom, RTMP, RTP (unicast or multicast), RTSP, UDP, Sat-IP, and Smooth Streaming, ensuring a smooth and flexible streaming experience.
Diverse Streaming Formats: Enjoy content in various streaming formats such as Apple HLS, Flash RTMP, MPEG-DASH, MPEG Transport Stream, RTP/RTSP ISMA/3GPP PSS, and Windows Media MMS.
EPG Support: The application also supports EPG in XMLTV format, allowing you to access program schedules and information conveniently.
User-Friendly Interface: The application boasts an attractive and user-friendly layout, enhancing your overall interaction and navigation.
It's important to note:
The application does not provide IPTV services, including subscriptions or streams. Users are required to have their own content sources. This approach emphasizes user autonomy and content ownership.

In essence, the IPTV application transforms Android devices into versatile entertainment hubs, offering a comprehensive suite of features for IPTV, VOD, EPG, and more. Whether you're enjoying content from your service provider or exploring free live TV channels, this application provides a powerful and convenient platform for your entertainment needs.
